Our Services

Rack space

Experience unparalleled reliability and security with our dedicated rack space service, providing customised power solutions tailored to your needs. Each rack offers a generous capacity of 42U, ensuring ample space for your equipment and enabling seamless scalability as your business grows.

Server rack cage/ Secure Cage with Multi Factor Authentication

Elevate your security standards with our Server Rack Cage service, featuring customizable options for opaque, solid privacy panels or complete mesh cages from floor to ceiling. Our self-closing doors utilise badge and biometric access, ensuring robust authentication measures for unparalleled data protection and compliance adherence. Experience peace of mind knowing your critical infrastructure is safeguarded by Anant Raj Data Centers' advanced security solutions.

Dedicated server hall/suite

Unlock the ultimate in control and security with our Dedicated Server Hall/Suite service—a data center within a data center, providing exclusive suites or rooms tailored to your specifications. Enjoy complete control over your infrastructure in a secure environment, coupled with the flexibility to scale as needed.

Dedicated server floor space

Maximise your control and flexibility with our Dedicated Server Floor Space service, offering full customization and security control over your cabling, power, and infrastructure. Benefit from the advantage of on-demand scalability, allowing you to expand or contract your footprint as your business needs evolve. Experience unparalleled freedom and efficiency with Anant Raj Data Centers' dedicated floor space solution tailored to your exact requirements.
